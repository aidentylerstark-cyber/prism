## Summary
Prism is a desktop application that connects your computer to the Second Life grid operated by
Linden Lab. Prism itself is not a service and does not require a Prism account.

## Data Prism collects
- **None required to install, run, or uninstall.** No Prism account is needed.
- **Login credentials** you enter are sent directly to the grid login service you choose; Prism
  does not transmit them to any Prism-operated server. Optional "remember password" stores them
  locally on your machine only.
- **Local settings, cache, and logs** are stored on your machine.

## Optional data
- **Crash reports / telemetry:** DISABLED by default. If enabled by you, we describe exactly what
  is sent (stack traces, viewer version, OS/GPU) and where. Nothing is sent without your explicit,
  informed opt-in.
- **Update checks:** Prism may contact Prism's own update endpoint to check for new versions. This
  is disclosed and can be disabled. Prism never contacts Linden Lab's official-viewer update service.

## Data sharing
- We do not sell or share collected data. We do not share any collected data with third parties
  without your explicit consent.

## Third-party services
- Interactions with the Second Life grid are governed by Linden Lab's own Terms of Service and
  Privacy Policy.
